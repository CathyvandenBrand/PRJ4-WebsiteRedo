Mission

While nature is slowing decaying and temperature slowly
start to raise we add blossom want to help people out to live greener
and cleaner lives. With starter packs with the essentials of recycling
and with clear descriptions how to plant more flowers.

Goals

  • With the money we will collect from the recycle packs we want to plant more trees, flowers and we want to help educate future generations with guest lectures how even they can make the world a better place already by throwing away trash and helping during clean-up/tree planting days.
  • Make people aware about their carbon footprint. Your carbon footprint is an indication how much you waste and how much place you take up on planet earth with your habits.
